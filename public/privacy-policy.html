01

Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how ENT Agency ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with the Creator Metrics application ("the App"), a multi-creator Instagram analytics dashboard accessible at creator-metrics.vercel.app.

This App is authorized by Meta Platforms, Inc. and accesses data through the Instagram Graph API and Meta Login platform. This policy governs all data obtained through those integrations.

Internal Tool Notice: Creator Metrics is a proprietary, invitation-only internal tool operated by ENT Agency. Access is restricted to authorized creators and staff. It is not a consumer-facing public application.

02

Who We Are

ENT Agency is a creator management agency specializing in health and wellness influencer marketing. We manage a curated roster of content creators across social media platforms and brand partnership programs.

This App was built to provide ENT Agency's internal team and managed creators with unified access to Instagram performance analytics, enabling data-driven content strategy and brand partnership reporting.

03

Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

Account & Authentication Data

  • Meta / Instagram user ID and username
  • OAuth 2.0 access tokens (encrypted at rest)
  • Instagram Business Account information

Instagram Analytics Data

  • Profile metrics (followers, following, profile views)
  • Media insights (reach, impressions, saves, comments, likes, shares)
  • Reel and story performance metrics
  • Audience demographics (age, gender, location — aggregated)
  • Account-level reach and engagement data

Technical / Usage Data

  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address (for security and authentication purposes)
  • App usage logs and session data

04

Instagram & Facebook Platform Data

The App uses the following Meta platform permissions and features:

  • instagram_basic
  • instagram_manage_insights
  • pages_show_list
  • pages_read_engagement
  • business_management
  • instagram_content_publish

Data accessed via these permissions is used exclusively for displaying analytics within the Creator Metrics dashboard. We do not use Instagram or Facebook data to build advertising audiences, sell data, or profile users for any purpose outside of the stated analytics functionality.

Permission Purpose
instagram_basic Read creator profile data and media for dashboard display
instagram_manage_insights Access post and account-level performance metrics
pages_show_list Identify connected Facebook Pages linked to Instagram accounts
pages_read_engagement Read Page insights for linked accounts
business_management Access Business Account data for multi-creator management
instagram_content_publish Publish content on behalf of managed creators for campaign deliverables

05

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect strictly for the following purposes:

  1. Analytics Dashboard: Display Instagram performance metrics to authorized creators and ENT Agency staff in a unified internal dashboard.
  2. Performance Reporting: Generate reports for brand partnership campaigns managed by ENT Agency.
  3. Content Strategy: Help creators and account managers identify high-performing content to inform future content decisions.
  4. Account Authentication: Verify identity and maintain secure, authorized access to the App.
  5. App Improvement: Monitor app performance, fix bugs, and improve reliability for our internal users.

We do not use your data for advertising, resale, training machine learning models, or any purpose not explicitly stated above.

06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • ENT Agency Internal Team: Authorized ENT Agency staff and account managers who require access to fulfill their management duties.
  • Infrastructure Providers: We use Vercel (hosting), Supabase (database), and Doppler (secrets management). These providers process data only as necessary to operate the App and are contractually bound to appropriate data handling standards.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ENT Agency or others.
Instagram and Facebook platform data is never transferred to third-party data brokers, advertisers, or analytics companies.

08

Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage of OAuth tokens via Doppler secrets management
  • Row-level security policies in our Supabase database
  • Access controls limiting data visibility to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of our authentication and data access flows

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is completely secure. If you become aware of any security concern, please contact us immediately at the address below.

09

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Revocation: Revoke Instagram/Facebook API access at any time via your Facebook App Settings. Revoking access will immediately prevent the App from accessing your Instagram data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at marketingteam@entagency.co. We will respond within 30 days.

Meta Platform Policy Compliance

Creator Metrics is built on Meta's platform and complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Meta Developer Policies, including:

  • We only request permissions necessary for the App's core functionality.
  • We do not use Instagram or Facebook data to build advertising audiences or shadow profiles.
  • We do not sell or license any data obtained through Meta APIs to third parties.
  • We provide users with a clear and accessible means to revoke data access.
  • We store platform data securely and delete it upon user request or account termination.
  • We do not use platform data for any purpose beyond what is disclosed in this policy and authorized by Meta.

Users can revoke the App's access to their Instagram data at any time by visiting Instagram's Authorized Apps settings or through their Facebook account settings.

If we become aware that a minor has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete that data promptly. If you believe a minor has accessed the App, please contact us immediately.
